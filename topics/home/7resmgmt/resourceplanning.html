Resource Planning

Resource planning page has portlets helps us to determine the Capacity and Demand information of a Resource along with Investment details and Actuals.

Other words - The Resource planning page has several portlets which provides information about Resource Allocations on monthly wise, Allocations on Projects wise, Capacity of the Roles, Resource requirements, Resource Bookings.

Below are the Tabs and portlets under the Resource planning page.

1. Capacity

This tab has 2 portlets

  • Role Capacity Histogram -report gives info on Roles, Associated Investment details, Capacity and Demand combined as a small Histogram graph.
  • Role Capacity - Report gives info on Roles, Associated Investment details, Numbers of Capacity and Demand as a separately.
2. Organizational Demand

This tab has 2 portlets

  • OBS Resource Aggregation -This portlet lists the aggregated demand for all Resources by OBS. (You can view allocation data at different levels within the selected OBS unit.)
  • OBS Invesment Aggregation - This portlet lists the aggregated demand for all Investment by OBS.(You can view allocation data at different levels within the selected OBS unit.)
3. Top down planning

This tab has 1 portlet

  • Top down planning by Invesment - This report provides information about the Investment name, Investment ID, associated Roles and their Allocation in terms of percentage.
4. Workloads

This tab has 1 portlet

  • Resource workloads - This portlet give information about the Resources, their Allocation and Availability rate as per Days, Week, Months, Quarter and Year.
5. Allocations

This tab has 2 portlets

  • Weekly Detail
  • Allocation Discrepancy
6.Unfilled Allocations

This tab has 1 portlet

  • Unfilled Requirements -This report shows data of Roles added in Invesments team tab ( unfilled requirements/demand) and it's booking status. Looking at the data helps us to fill the Roles with the approriate Resources who fulfils the roles.

    • This report also shows information like Role Allocation start and finish dates, Allocation in hours and the Allocation percentage.

7.Bookings

This tab has 1 portlet

  • Booking Status - This report provides info about Resources their associated Projects and the Booking status( from the Team tab). This portlet gives, Allocation start and finish dates, Resources Allocation between the dates and Allocation Percentages.

Navigation

Please follow the below steps

  • Navigate to Home tab
  • Under Resource management click on Resource Planning link
